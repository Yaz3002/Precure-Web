About Precure

Pretty Cure (プリキュア) is a Japanese magical girl anime franchise created by Izumi Todo and produced by Toei Animation. The series follows ordinary girls who transform into legendary warriors known as Pretty Cure (or Precure) to fight against evil forces and protect the world.

Running since 2004 with over 900 episodes

Features over 70 different Precure warriors across all seasons

Known for positive messages about friendship, courage, and perseverance

Learn More

Magical Girls with a Mission

Each season introduces new Precure warriors with unique powers and themes, from flowers and sweets to stars and music.

"Pretty Cure stands as one of the longest-running and most successful magical girl franchises in anime history."

Heartcatch Precure

2010 • 49 episodes

Tsubomi Hanasaki, a shy girl who loves flowers, becomes Cure Blossom and teams up with her new friend Erika who becomes Cure Marine to protect the Heart Tree from the Desert Apostles.

Go! Princess Precure

2015 • 50 episodes

Haruka Haruno enrolls in Noble Academy to follow her dream of becoming a princess. There she becomes Cure Flora and joins other Precures to protect everyone's dreams from being locked away.
